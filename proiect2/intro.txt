Welcome to Naughts and Crosses! v1.0

The game is very simple,
Try to get 3 in a row

Balance between blocking your opponent
and playing offensive to win

How to play:

The board starts like this:

- - -
- - -
- - -

You enter your positions and they change

for example:

Player 1 inputs: 5

The board changes to:

- - -
- X -
- - -

Player 2 inputs : 9

- - -
- X -
- - 0
